# Outbreaks General Playbook

## Risk Bands
- 0-24: Low
- 25-49: Guarded
- 50-74: Elevated
- 75-100: Critical

## Action Playbook
### Low (0-24)
- Monitor routine surveillance feeds weekly.
- Maintain baseline communications with local partners.
- No resource reallocation required.

### Guarded (25-49)
- Increase monitoring cadence to 2-3 times per week.
- Validate recent case reports and lab confirmations.
- Prepare communication templates for rapid public updates.

### Elevated (50-74)
- Daily monitoring of case trends and hospital capacity.
- Coordinate with regional public health for targeted interventions.
- Pre-position medical supplies and staffing plans.

### Critical (75-100)
- Activate incident response structure.
- Initiate surge capacity protocols and public guidance.
- Coordinate cross-jurisdictional response and resource mobilization.
